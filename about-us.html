Project

49 Cyclists is a place where UNC Charlotte students and faculty can find helpful resources about cycling in North Charlotte area. This includes links to North Carolina traffic laws, articles on health benefits and recommendations about cycling gears and accessories.

UNC Charlotte

The University of North Carolina at Charlotte (UNC Charlotte, UNCC, or simply Charlotte) is a public research university in Charlotte, North Carolina. It has three campuses: Charlotte Research Institute Campus, Center City Campus, and the main campus, located in University City. UNC Charlotte offers 23 doctoral, 64 master's, and 140 bachelor's degree programs through nine colleges. It is the largest institution of higher education in the Charlotte region.
